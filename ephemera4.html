The theme of the ticket Subjective Time is mortality, and whereas the conventional ticket might indicate time paid for, this piece records the fact that my years are passing more quickly. The My 2014 serial number is a reference to the Italian mailartist and brilliant self-historificationist, G.A. Cavellini who predicted his death in 2014 and died before his time. My Card, The Late Keith Bates, is a more light hearted take on death. The Mystique Docket is a mailart good luck charm. Ticket X evokes a scientific power, or else it's just a ticket with an identity crisis.
